# Contoso, Ltd. -- Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) Policy

**Document owner:** Contoso Information Technology
**Policy ID:** IT-BYOD-004
**Effective date:** January 1, 2025
**Applies to:** All Contoso employees who use a personal device for work

## 1. Purpose

This policy explains the rules for using a personally owned phone, tablet, or laptop to access Contoso
email, files, and applications. The goal is to let employees work from the devices they prefer while
keeping Contoso data protected.

## 2. Eligibility and enrollment

Any full-time employee may enroll a personal device. Before a personal device can reach Contoso
resources, the employee must enroll it in Contoso's mobile device management (**MDM**) system. MDM is
the service that lets IT apply security settings and, if needed, remove company data from a device.
Enrollment takes about ten minutes and is self-service through the company portal app.

## 3. Minimum security requirements

Every enrolled device must meet all of the following:

| Requirement | Standard |
|---|---|
| Screen lock | A PIN, password, or biometric unlock, locking after 5 minutes of inactivity |
| Operating system | Updated to a version still receiving security patches from the vendor |
| Disk encryption | Turned on for the whole device |
| Antimalware | Required on personal laptops; built-in mobile protection is sufficient on phones and tablets |

A device that falls out of compliance loses access to Contoso resources until the employee fixes the issue.

## 4. Monthly stipend

Contoso reimburses approved BYOD participants a **$40 per month** stipend toward the personal phone plan
they use for work. The stipend is added to the regular paycheck and requires no receipts. Laptops and
tablets are not eligible for the stipend.

## 5. Separation of company and personal data

Contoso can manage and remove only company data on a personal device. IT cannot see an employee's
personal photos, messages, or browsing history. When an employee leaves Contoso or unenrolls a device,
IT performs a **selective wipe** that removes company email, files, and apps while leaving personal
data untouched.

## 6. Lost or stolen devices

Report a lost or stolen enrolled device to the IT service desk within **24 hours**. IT will issue a
selective wipe of Contoso data from the device remotely.

## 7. Questions

For anything this document does not cover, contact the Contoso IT service desk at **ithelp@contoso.com**.
