--- Sync vs. async skills ---
Sync (return immediately):
  recognize_entities        -- typed named entities + categories
  recognize_pii_entities    -- PII detection + redaction
  extract_key_phrases       -- salient terms (untyped)
  analyze_sentiment         -- doc + sentence + aspect (opinion mining)
  detect_language           -- ISO language code + confidence
Async LRO (return a poller -- call .result()):
  begin_extract_summary     -- verbatim source sentences
  begin_abstract_summary    -- paraphrased novel sentences

--- Aspect-based sentiment ---
analyze_sentiment(docs, show_opinion_mining=True) -> 3 tiers:
  1. Document sentiment   (positive/negative/neutral/mixed)
  2. Sentence sentiment
  3. Mined opinions       (target, assessment) pairs explaining WHY
For AI-901 today:
  show_opinion_mining=True is THE answer.

--- Extractive vs. abstractive summary ---
Auditable / compliance / legal       -> Extractive  (begin_extract_summary)
Reads like human prose / exec digest -> Abstractive (begin_abstract_summary)
